Whisper Guard

Privacy, in one paragraph

We only ever see a site's name, never your history, never the page. When you are signed in, the hostname of the page you visit is sent to graph.whisper.security to answer the live safety check, and nothing else: no path, no query, no content, no identifiers beyond your own key. Hostnames are used only to answer the check and are not retained to build a browsing profile. The on-device look-alike protection never sends anything at all. There is no telemetry and no analytics in this extension.
