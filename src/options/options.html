Privacy, in one paragraph

We only ever see a site's name, never your history, never the page. The live safety check sends the hostname of the page you visit to graph.whisper.security, whether or not you have an account, and nothing else: no path, no query, no content. Hostnames are used only to answer the check and are not retained to build a browsing profile. The on-device look-alike protection sends nothing at all, and one switch below turns the live check off entirely. Endpoint identity checks send only IP literals of your own endpoints to rdap.whisper.online. There is no telemetry and no analytics in this extension.
